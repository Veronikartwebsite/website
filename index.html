VeronikArt Australia

Water Color, Murals, Pastel, Oil, Pyrography - Open for Commissions

Veronika is a painter, pyrographer and all art genres lover based in Mandurah, Australia.

She gets her inspiration from everyday life and encounters, and her dreams. She can produce a portrait from a picture, paint a beautiful landscape, immortalise your pet in a painting and is open to any art projects you might need done, like if you want your house wall decorated.

Some of her art will be put up on Artfinder for sale. Other pieces could also be sold and the price is negotiable. Don't hesitate to contact Veronika if you have any question about her talents and art.
